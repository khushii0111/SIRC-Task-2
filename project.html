GMUN

What it is: GMUN is a simulation of the United Nations, where students take on the roles of diplomats, representing different countries and engaging in debates on global issues.
Goals: The primary goal of GMUN is to educate students about international relations, diplomacy, and the United Nations agenda, while also fostering skills like public speaking, critical thinking, and negotiation.

Global Model United Nations (GMUN) conferences allow participants to master diplomacy, public speaking, and global leadership through online or live UN simulations. Students take on roles representing countries, organizations, or individuals to address global challenges. Model UN simulations are a great way to learn about the UN's principles and functions.
